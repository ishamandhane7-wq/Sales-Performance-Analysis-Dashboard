# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive Sales Performance Dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI. The goal is to analyze business performance across revenue, profit, regions, and product categories.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall revenue and profit performance
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Evaluate regional sales contribution
- Understand revenue trends over time
- Assess profitability across different segments

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Excel (Data Source)

---

## 📁 Project Files
- sales-performance-dashboard.pbix
- Super-Store-Sales-Data.csv
- screenshots/dashboard.png

---

## 📊 Key KPIs
- Total Revenue: $13M
- Total Profit: $1.47M
- Total Units Sold: 178K
- Profit Margin: 11.62%

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Technology category is the top-performing segment in both revenue and profit.
- Furniture shows relatively lower profitability despite decent sales.
- Central region contributes the highest revenue.
- Phones, Copiers, and Chairs are top-selling products.
- Revenue shows a steady upward trend over time.
- Profitability varies across categories, highlighting the importance of margin-focused strategies.
